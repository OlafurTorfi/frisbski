Frisbski

Frisbski is an alpin skiing and frisbee discipline, involving throwing and catching a frisbee while skiing down slopes.

This website is the official website for information and promotion of the sport.

History

In 2012 Einar Beinteinn Árnason and Ólafur Torfi Yngvason started playing with a frisbee while on a skitrip to Hlíðarfjall Akureyri.

In early 2015 the Frisbski website was launched.

Goals

To spread knowledge about Frisbski

To make the official frisbski disk allowed in as many skislopes as possible (or prevent it from being banned
